Who am I?

My name is Richard.

I am a front end web developer and UX designer based in the beautiful coast of Australia, Sydney.

I really enjoy solving problems as well as making things pretty and easy to use. I can't stop learning new things; the more, the better. I also love drawing, a hobby I'm taking along since the day I was born. Oh, and I really like milk; I have a passion for milk! :)



What do I do?

I'm a programmer.

For the front-end I usually work with Javascript, either standalone or including popular frameworks like ReactJS etc. I also make the web pretty by using CSS and, whenever needed, any of their friends: Bootstrap, Bulma, etc.

For the back-end I also work with Javascript and Python. But, of course, whenever the project requires PHP, I do PHP as well (Wordpress, Laravel, etc).

Also a designer.

I feel comfortable working Adobe products such as Photoshop or Illistrator are some kind of industry standards and I love working with them. I'm not limited to them, though: Gimp, Krita and similar digital art applications are also apps that I've been working with.

User interfaces, brochures, books, branding... You name it! As I mentioned, creating pretty things is particularly important for me! :)
